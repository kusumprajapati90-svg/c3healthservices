Step-by-Step: Changing a Catheter Bag Safely

Maintaining a sterile closed-drainage system is critical. While it can be done at home, many families prefer professional assistance to ensure clinical safety.

1

Sanitization

Thoroughly wash hands and use sterile gloves to prevent bacteria from entering the system.

2

Disconnection

Clean the connection point with an alcohol swab before carefully disconnecting the old bag.

3

Attachment

Immediately connect the sterile new bag, ensuring the tip does not touch any unsterile surfaces.

4

Positioning

Secure the bag below the level of the bladder to ensure proper gravity-led drainage.
