Expert Maintenance Checklist

Follow these clinical steps to maintain a healthy urinary drainage system and ensure patient comfort.

Daily Hygiene

Clean the catheter insertion site twice daily with mild soap and water. Always wash your hands before and after touching any part of the system.

Gravity Management

Ensure the drainage bag is always kept below the level of the bladder to prevent urine backflow, which can cause serious infections.

Observation

Monitor urine color, clarity, and volume. Watch for kinks in the tubing and report any leakage or blockages to a professional immediately.
